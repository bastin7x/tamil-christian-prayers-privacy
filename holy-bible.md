Information We Collect
We DO NOT Collect Personal Information
Tamil Bible Compare+ is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Local Data Storage
The app stores the following information locally on your device only:
✅ User Preferences — Your theme selection (Light / Dark mode)
✅ Language Preference — Your chosen language (Tamil / English)
✅ Selected Translation — Your preferred Bible translation (e.g., Tamil BSI, KJV, ESV)
✅ Bookmarks — Bible verses you have bookmarked, including any personal notes you added
✅ Bookmark Labels — Custom labels you have created (e.g., "Jesus", "Sunday Prayer", "Meeting")
✅ Last Read Position — The book, chapter, and verse you last read
This data is stored using your device's local storage (SQLite database / SharedPreferences) and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or the app developer.

Tamil Bible Compare+ is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Data Security
Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.
All data stored on your device (bookmarks, labels, preferences, reading position) is protected by your device's own security features and is accessible only by this app.

Data Retention and Deletion
Local Data

All app data is stored locally on your device
You can clear all app data at any time through:
Device Settings → Apps → Tamil Bible Compare+ → Storage → Clear Data
Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data, including all bookmarks and labels

Export Feature
The app provides an Export Bookmarks feature that allows you to save your bookmarks as a JSON file to your device's local storage. This file stays on your device and is never sent anywhere automatically.
No Cloud Storage
We do not store any of your data on our servers or in the cloud.

Your Rights
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data for us to share, correct, or delete on your behalf.
However, you have complete control over all local data stored on your device and can delete it at any time as described above.
