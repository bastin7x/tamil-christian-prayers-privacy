## Information We Collect

### We DO NOT Collect Personal Information

Tamil Christian Prayers is designed with your privacy in mind. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.**

Specifically, we do NOT collect:
- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
- ❌ Crash reports or diagnostic data
- ❌ Browsing history or app usage patterns
- ❌ Contact lists or photos
- ❌ Any other personal or sensitive information

### Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information **locally on your device only**:
- ✅ **User Preferences:** Your theme selection (Light/Dark mode)
- ✅ **Language Preference:** Your chosen language (Tamil/English)
- ✅ **Daily Checklist Progress:** Your prayer completion status for the daily checklist feature

This data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences and **never leaves your device**. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or the app developer.

**Important:** This permission does NOT mean we collect or transmit your data. The app functions **completely offline** and does not require an internet connection to work.
